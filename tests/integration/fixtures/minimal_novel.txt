Chapter 1: A Brief Encounter

Anna sat at the cafe, stirring her coffee slowly. The rain outside painted gray streaks on the window.

"You came," said Luca, sliding into the seat across from her. His dark hair was damp from the rain.

Anna looked up. "I almost didn't."

They sat in silence for a moment, listening to the rain. There was so much to say, and yet the words felt impossible.

"I'm leaving tomorrow," Luca finally said. "I wanted to say goodbye properly."

Anna nodded. "I know. Take care of yourself, Luca."

He reached across the table and squeezed her hand. Then he stood, pulled on his jacket, and walked out into the rain. Anna watched him go, her coffee growing cold.
