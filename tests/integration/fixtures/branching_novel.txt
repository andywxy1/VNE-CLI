Chapter 1: The Crossroads

Kira stood at the fork in the forest path, her red cloak billowing in the cold wind. The left path led to the village of Ashenmoor, where safety and warmth awaited. The right path descended into the Whispering Gorge, where strange lights had been spotted for weeks.

Her companion Theo adjusted his spectacles and pulled his coat tighter. "We should head to the village," he said nervously. "My research can wait."

Kira shook her head. "People are disappearing, Theo. If we don't investigate now, more will vanish."

Chapter 2: Consequences

Regardless of which path was chosen, Kira and Theo reunited at the old tavern in Ashenmoor three days later. Both carried new knowledge, and the mystery was deeper than either had imagined.

"The lights in the gorge," Kira began, setting a strange glowing stone on the table. "They're coming from these. I found dozens of them scattered along the ravine."

Theo leaned forward, his scholarly instincts overtaking his fear. "Fascinating. This matches descriptions from the old archives. These are memory stones -- they record events and play them back."

"Then someone is trying to preserve something," Kira said. "Or reveal it."
